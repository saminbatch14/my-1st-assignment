When and where is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 will take place this fall. The exact venue and dates will be announced soon on this page.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Yes, tickets are refundable up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to a colleague.

Yes, Pro and Team ticket holders get access to recordings of all sessions after the event.

Yes, students with a valid ID get 50% off the Standard ticket. Contact us for the discount code.
